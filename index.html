The Band

The band resides in their hometown Portales, New Mexico. There, they all attend Eastern New Mexico University where they are persuing majors in music. Stage Fright made their first apperance at Parker and Nacho's Junior High talent show. Later, in college, they met Stage Frights newest member, Aryn and immediately hit it off. At first the band was scared to perfom, and you could say they had a horrible case of stage fright, giving them their name. Now these multi talented musicians are putting their college classes on hold to perform their new album on their very first tour!!!!

Guitarist: Parker Yi

Parker is 22 years old, and a senior at Eastern New Mexico University. He has been in band at Portales since 6th grade along with his best friend Nacho. He was inspired by many major rock bands, and always dreamed of it being him up on that stage. Now Parker is bringing riffs and solos that shake the ground!

Bassist: Nacho Perez

Nacho is 22 years old, and is also a senior at ENMU. He is very dedicated to music, and dreams of being a professional musician. He is the band's lead singer most of the time, and writes a lot of the band's original songs. He plays grooves that get the crowd moving every time.

Drummer: Aryn Brown

Aryn is from Roswell, New Mexico. She is 21 and has always had a passion for music. Aryn has played the drums since high school and found it was her calling. She attends college in Portales, NM at Eastern New Mexico University and is persuing a degree in music. Now she's pounding the drums like a storm, the heart of the band.
